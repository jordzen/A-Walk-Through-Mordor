A Walk Through Mordor

"One does not simply walk into Mordor...".

Frodo has fallen and passed the ring onto you.

Do you have what it takes to venture through the dark lands of mordor and destroy the ring of power?

To win the game, you must pass four stages, each increasing in difficulty.

Tread carful, as Mordor is never empty.

RULES

  • There is only ONE correct card per stage, starting at stage one, at the bottom of the page.
  • Click on the cards to reveal them.
  • If you choose the CORRECT card, the card will turn GREEN - move to the next stage.
  • If you choose the WRONG card, the card will turn RED - you must roll a D20 dice to save your journey and try again to find the correct card on the SAME STAGE.
  • Each stage adds an extra card, making the percentage of picking the correct card lower.
  • Find all the correct cards to win the game.
